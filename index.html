Sepsis is one of the most underrecognized medical emergencies affecting nearly 50 million people[2] worldwide each year.

The purpose of this project is to provide a comprehensive collection of sepsis information. Sepsis is an immune overreaction to an infection[1]. While you might have heard of it as being referred to as "blood poisoning", those are technically separate things. A septic response is triggered by an infection, similar to how a bee sting might trigger an allergic response.
